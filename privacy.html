Privacy Policy

CarbonPocket — Carbon Footprint Tracker

Global Data Protection Compliance (GDPR, LGPD, CCPA)

Last Updated: July 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how CarbonPocket ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To provide our carbon tracking and offsetting services, we collect the following categories of data:

2.1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Data: Age range and general location (Country/City).
  • Activity Data: Information manually entered regarding transportation modes, distances traveled, food consumption patterns, energy utility bills, flight details, and purchase history.

2.2. Optional Information (Requires Explicit Consent)

  • Contacts: If you choose to use social features, we access your phone contacts to identify friends already using CarbonPocket.

2.3. Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Analytics: Information on how you interact with the app, including screens viewed, features used, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

3. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

We prioritize data minimization. CarbonPocket does not collect:

  • Precise GPS location or exact home addresses.
  • Government-issued identification numbers.
  • Financial account or credit card numbers (all transactions are processed securely via Apple App Store or Google Play Store).
  • Biometric or sensitive health data.

4. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We process your data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: Calculating your carbon footprint and providing personalized reduction recommendations via our AI assistant.
  • Social Features: Enabling friend comparisons, rankings, and community challenges.
  • Offsetting Management: Processing your carbon offset selections and generating exportable ESG reports.
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to optimize features and user experience.
  • Communication: Sending essential service updates and, if opted-in, sustainability tips.

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: If you use social features, your name, profile picture, and carbon footprint data (monthly/yearly totals and streaks) will be visible to your connected friends.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third parties for cloud hosting (e.g., AWS/Google Cloud) and analytics (e.g., Firebase).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the services. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings.

7. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR, LGPD, CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request the rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request the permanent removal of your personal data.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Revoke permissions for contact access or health integrations at any time.
  • Non-Discrimination: California residents have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising their CCPA rights.

8. SECURITY MEASURES

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS/SSL for data in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

CarbonPocket is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information immediately.

10. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: info@carbonpocket.app
